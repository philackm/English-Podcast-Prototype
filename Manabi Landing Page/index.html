Timeline

Every podcast comes with a timeline, transcriptions and timestamps allowing you to quickly jump back and forward through the podcast to that line you just couldn’t catch.

Definitions

Learn English in English! Simple, easy to understand definitions in English appear right next to the line you’re currently listening to. You can toggle the Japanese translation too, just in case!

Content

Podcasts by English teachers with years of experience teaching Japanese students. Covering a wide range of topics, there are seasons that are sure to be of interest. New podcasts are released tri-weekly!
